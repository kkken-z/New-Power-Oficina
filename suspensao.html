Suspension

The suspension is one of the most important systems for the good performance and safety of your vehicle. She is responsible for absorbing the impacts of terrain irregularities, providing smooth driving smoother and more comfortable, in addition to maintaining the car's stability during the journey. When the suspension is in good condition, you feel the difference in driving comfort and safety, especially when cornering and braking.

Composed of several components such as shock absorbers, springs, bushings and stabilizer bars, the suspension has the function of keeping the tires in constant contact with the ground, which is crucial for the vehicle stability. A well-calibrated suspension system also helps ensure wear and tear uniformity of the tires, preventing them from wearing unevenly, which could affect grip and performance. vehicle performance.

Suspension maintenance must be done regularly, as the components in this part of the car are exposed to constant wear due to factors such as the condition of the roads, the weight of the vehicle and the driving style. Worn shock absorbers and springs can affect the car's ability to absorb impacts and maintain stability, especially during sudden braking or when passing through holes and speed bumps.

In addition to improving comfort and safety, a well-maintained suspension can also impact positively improve drivability and fuel consumption. This is because when the suspension is not working properly, the car may become more difficult to control, requiring more driver and engine effort to maintain speed and stability.

To ensure that your vehicle's suspension is always in perfect condition, it is important to carry out periodic checks. If you notice that the car is having difficulty absorbing impacts, if there are strange noises, if the steering is stiffer or if the car tends to "jump" more than usual, it may be a sign that it is time to review the suspension system.

Investing in preventive suspension maintenance is essential for your safety and comfort when driving. drive. The team of qualified professionals will be ready to carry out the complete review and ensure that your vehicle is always ready to face any road with confidence.
